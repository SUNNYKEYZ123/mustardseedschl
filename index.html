Welcome to Mustard Seed International Schools!!

Established in 1995, Mustard Seed International Group of Schools has proudly stood at the forefront of educational excellence, dedicated to nurturing young minds for over 30years. Our institution is rooted in a rich tradition of combining rigorous academic standards with holistic character development, ensuring that students grow intellectually, morally, and socially.

At Mustard Seed, we believe education is more than just acquiring knowledgeâ€”it is about shaping well-rounded individuals who are prepared to thrive in a rapidly evolving global society. Our comprehensive curriculum is designed to ignite curiosity, foster creativity, and encourage critical thinking, while our vibrant extracurricular programs promote leadership, teamwork, and community engagement.

Our mission is to nurture curious, compassionate, and confident individuals who are equipped with the skills, values, and resilience needed to meet the challenges of an ever-changing world. We are committed to creating a safe, inclusive, and stimulating environment where every student is empowered to reach their full potential and become a positive influence in their communities. As we continue to grow and evolve, Mustard Seed International Group of Schools remains dedicated to excellence, innovation, and the holistic development of our students, preparing them not only for academic success but for lifelong achievement and meaningful contributions to society..
